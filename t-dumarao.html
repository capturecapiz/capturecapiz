    Brief Description

                Dumarao, Capiz, is a town steeped in rich history and vibrant culture, largely shaped by its Indigenous Peoples (IP’s). The town's IP communities, with their deep-rooted traditions, crafts, and folklore, contribute greatly to the cultural fabric of the region. Visitors have the unique opportunity to immerse themselves in these communities’ customs, arts, and rituals, which have been preserved through generations. The preservation of their heritage makes Dumarao a cultural hub for those interested in exploring the traditions of the region's native people.

                In addition to its cultural heritage, Dumarao is also known for its stunning natural landscapes, particularly its breathtaking waterfalls. Nestled in the town's lush forests, these natural wonders offer a serene escape into nature, providing a tranquil environment for visitors seeking adventure or relaxation. The waterfalls, along with the town's vibrant flora and fauna, highlight Dumarao’s potential for eco-tourism, drawing nature enthusiasts from all over. With its rich blend of cultural and natural attractions, Dumarao is a destination that truly showcases the best of Capiz’s heritage and natural beauty.
