Where to Dine in Capiz

A Culinary Journey Through the Seafood Capital

ESPACIO VERDE RESORT

Espacio Verde Resort features two distinct dining venues: Abuelo Restaurant and Aquatico Restaurant...
Seafood

Baybay Seafoods Restaurant

Experience the freshest catch of the day, cooked to perfection. A go-to place for an authentic Capiz seafood feast.
Barbecue

Bitoy's Balay Barbekyuhan

A casual dining spot specializing in grilled meats and seafood, perfect for a hearty and satisfying meal.
Filipino

RML Manokan Haus

Famous for its classic chicken inasal and other beloved Filipino comfort foods in a casual, family-friendly setting.
Filipino Grill

Jacque's Food & Drinks

Known for its lively atmosphere and delicious, affordable Filipino barbecue and classic dishes.
Native Filipino

Enting's Native Food

Offers an authentic taste of traditional Capiznon and Filipino dishes in a homey, rustic setting.
Modern Filipino

The Story Kitchen

Focuses on telling a story through its creative and well-presented dishes, often using local ingredients.
Filipino Buffet

Hukad sa Golden Cowrie

A popular chain offering a feast of unlimited rice and celebrated Filipino dishes for the whole family.
Filipino Feast

Fiesta! Fiesta!

Live up to its name by offering a festive atmosphere and a wide variety of Filipino favorites, perfect for celebrations.
Spanish-Filipino

Spanggo Cafe & Deli

A charming spot offering a fusion of Spanish and Filipino dishes, great coffee, pastries, and deli products.
Fusion

Acceso

A modern and stylish restaurant offering a creative menu that fuses local ingredients with international flavors.
Pizza & Pasta

Pyt Stop

A popular hangout for its delicious pizzas, pasta, and casual American-inspired comfort food.
Pizza

Pizza Junction

Another local favorite for pizza lovers, serving a variety of classic and unique pizza creations.
Casual Dining

Tracy's

A well-known diner-style restaurant serving a mix of Filipino and Western dishes that appeal to all ages.
Japanese

Arashi's Takoyaki & Ramen

Your go-to spot in Roxas for authentic Japanese favorites like takoyaki, ramen, and gyoza.
Cafe & Restaurant

Cafe Terraza

Offers stunning hilltop views of the city, along with a menu of Filipino and international dishes, making it ideal for romantic dinners.
Dessert Cafe

Panulce

The perfect place to satisfy your sweet tooth, offering a delightful selection of cakes, pastries, and desserts.
Themed Cafe

Cafe 1947

A historical-themed cafe that provides a cozy ambiance, good food, and a glimpse into the past.
Cafe & Bakeshop

Amelia's Cafe

A cozy and relaxing cafe known for its delicious cakes, pastries, and light meals. Perfect for an afternoon break.
Bakeshop

Bread & Butter

A beloved local bakeshop chain famous for its affordable and delicious breads, pastries, and pasalubong items.
