ACTIVITIES FOR THE MONTH OF JANUARY

January in Capiz

Binuligay Festival

Jamindan, Capiz

The Binuligay Festival celebrates the spirit of giving and generosity among the people of Jamindan. It's a colorful and joyous occasion that showcases the town's culture and traditions through street parades and cultural performances.

Hil-o Hil-o Festival

Maayon, Capiz

This festival celebrates the time-honored Filipino tradition of "bayanihan" or community cooperation. "Hil-o Hil-o" translates to "to help one another," reflecting the strong community ties that bind the people of Ma-ayon.

Dinagsa Festival

Dumalag, Capiz

The Dinagsa Festival is a religious celebration honoring the town's patron saint. It is highlighted by a fluvial parade and vibrant street performances that draw crowds from all over the province.

Sirinadya Festival

Tapaz, Capiz

The Sirinadya Festival is a showcase of the rich culture and traditions of the indigenous people of Tapaz. The festival features authentic ethnic dances, music, and rituals, providing a unique glimpse into the town's heritage.
