    Brief Description

                Maayon is a town nestled in the central part of Capiz, known for its fertile agricultural land that produces rice, corn, and coconut. This municipality is home to a community deeply connected to the land, with farming being the main livelihood for most of its residents. Its natural beauty, marked by rolling hills, rivers, and lush green fields, attracts eco-tourists and nature lovers alike. The town also celebrates various cultural festivals that highlight the rich heritage and traditions passed down through generations.

                In addition to its agricultural significance, Maayon has historical landmarks that reflect its contributions to Capiz’s growth. The people of Maayon take great pride in their community's accomplishments and the preservation of their customs. The town's combination of agricultural prosperity, natural beauty, and historical importance makes it an integral part of Capiz.
