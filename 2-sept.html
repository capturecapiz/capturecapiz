ACTIVITIES FOR THE MONTH OF SEPTEMBER

September in Capiz

Tourism Month Celebration

Province-wide

A month-long celebration dedicated to promoting the province's tourism assets, with special travel deals, tours, and events to attract visitors.

Philippine Creative Industries Month

Province-wide

A celebration of Capiz's creative talents, from artisans to performers, as mandated by Republic Act No. 11904.

Buyloganay Festival

Ivisan, Capiz

"Buyloganay" means "to help one another," and this festival celebrates the strong cooperative spirit of the people of Ivisan.

Pasalamat Festival

Dao, Capiz

A grand festival of thanksgiving ("pasalamat") for a bountiful harvest and blessings received by the town.

Patabang Festival

Tapaz, Capiz

"Patabang" means to help, and this festival highlights the bayanihan spirit of the people of Tapaz through various community-centered activities.
