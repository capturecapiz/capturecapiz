    Filamer Christian University: A Legacy of Faith and Education

                Nestled in the heart of Roxas City, Filamer Christian University is more than just a premier educational institution; it is a historic landmark and a testament to a legacy of faith that has shaped Capiz for over a century. Founded in 1904 by American Baptist missionaries, its name beautifully captures its mission: Fil-American partnership.

                 The university began as a small orphanage and has since blossomed into a sprawling campus that blends modern facilities with historic, early 20th-century colonial architecture. For tourists interested in history and architecture, the campus itself is a destination. As you walk through its grounds, you can see beautifully preserved buildings that echo the American colonial era, offering a picturesque glimpse into the city's past.

                The campus is a peaceful and inspiring environment, with shaded walkways and open spaces that welcome visitors for a quiet stroll. It stands as a proud symbol of the city's commitment to education and its deep historical ties. A visit to Filamer Christian University offers a unique look at an institution that has not only educated generations of Capiznons but has also been an integral part of the city's cultural and historical landscape.
