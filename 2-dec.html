ACTIVITIES FOR THE MONTH OF DECEMBER

December in Capiz

Sinadya Festival

Roxas City

Formerly known as Sinadya sa Halaran, this is the grandest festival of Roxas City. It is a celebration of joy and thanksgiving, marked by a fluvial parade, street dancing, and various cultural shows.

Paskwa sang Maragtas: Capiz Christmas Festival

Province-wide

A province-wide Christmas celebration that features festive decorations, parol-making contests, and a series of holiday events that light up the entire province.

Anniversary of the Liberation of Capiz and Veterans Memorial Day

Province-wide

A day to honor and remember the war veterans and the historic liberation of the province. Commemorative ceremonies are held to pay tribute to their heroism.
