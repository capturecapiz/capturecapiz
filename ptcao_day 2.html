Capiz Short Tour:

A Journey Through History, Culture, and Nature

Embark on a captivating 2-day journey through the heart of Capiz, where history, culture, and nature converge. Day 1 immerses you in the province's rich heritage as you visit iconic landmarks, historical sites, and vibrant local communities. Savor authentic Capisnon hospitality through eco-tours and culinary delights. Day 2 takes you on a countryside adventure, from serene beaches and pilgrimage sites to caves and cultural villages. This tour offers a perfect blend of exploration, relaxation, and meaningful connections with Capiz's people and traditions, leaving you with unforgettable memories and treasures to bring home.

Sites to be visit:

Day 1: A Journey into Capiz's Rich History and Culture
Morning: Explore Capiz's Heritage

Birthplace of President Manuel A. Roxas
Visit the ancestral house of President Manuel A. Roxas, a historical site that celebrates the life and legacy of the first President of the Philippine Republic. It showcases artifacts and memorabilia that highlight his role in shaping the nation's history.


President Manuel A. Roxas Monument
This monument stands as a tribute to Roxas City's most prominent figure, symbolizing his contributions to the country's independence and development.


Roxas City Bandstand
A charming venue in the heart of the city, the Bandstand is a significant cultural site where locals gather for musical performances and public events.


Immaculate Conception Cathedral
This iconic cathedral is a masterpiece of Spanish colonial architecture. Its serene atmosphere and intricate design make it an essential stop for history and art enthusiasts.


Provincial Capitol
A landmark that reflects the grandeur of Capiz's governance, the Provincial Capitol is an architectural gem with a rich historical narrative.


Sta. Monica Church & Panay Bell
Visit this centuries-old church, home to Asia's largest bell cast from 70 sacks of coins. Its historical and spiritual significance draws pilgrims and tourists alike.


Ang Panublion Museum
Discover Capiz's vibrant culture and history at this museum, featuring exhibits on local art, traditions, and the province's Spanish-era legacy.


Day 1: A Journey into Capiz's Rich History and Culture
Noon: Savor the Capisnon Hospitality

Palina Greenbelt Eco-Park/Cadimahan River Tour
Indulge in a relaxing lunch while enjoying the serene views of the river and lush mangroves. The eco-park promotes sustainability, offering floating cottages and a glimpse into the community's harmonious relationship with nature.


Day 1: A Journey into Capiz's Rich History and Culture
Afternoon: Commune with the Locals

Dumolog Dried Fish Community
Witness the traditional methods of fish drying—a livelihood passed through generations. This visit offers a glimpse into Capiz’s coastal life.


Kapis Atbp.
Learn about Capiz shell processing and admire the craftsmanship behind intricate handicrafts. Shop for unique souvenirs that embody local artistry.


Banica Dried Fish Pasalubong Center
Stock up on dried seafood products, a staple in Capiz's pasalubong culture. The vibrant market scene highlights the region's fishing industry.


Day 2: Discover the Countryside and Nature's Wonders
Morning: Countryside Excursion

Basiao Beach, Ivisan
Experience island hopping along the pristine waters of Basiao Beach. Its unspoiled beauty and peaceful surroundings make it perfect for relaxation and exploration.


Our Lady of Lourdes Grotto Meditation Hills, Sapian
Find spiritual tranquility at this pilgrimage site. The grotto's scenic views and meditative ambiance provide a serene escape.


Day 2: Discover the Countryside and Nature's Wonders
Noon: Nature and Heritage Escape

Suhot Cave and Spring
Enjoy a hearty lunch amidst nature's beauty. Engage in spelunking, swimming, and trekking at Suhot Cave and Spring, a site that combines adventure with breathtaking landscapes.


St. Martin of Tours Church
Marvel at the intricate details of this heritage church, a testament to Capiz's deep-rooted religious history and architectural grandeur.


Day 2: Immerse with Nature
Afternoon: Harmony with Nature

Capiz Eco-Park and Cultural Village
Explore the eco-park's lush trails and immerse yourself in Capiznon culture. Indulge in native delicacies, learn about local history, and participate in health and wellness activities.


Day 2: Souvenir Shopping
Afternoon: Take home a piece of Capiz through delightful pasalubong items, from local delicacies to intricately crafted souvenirs.

Capiz Tourist Information Center and Specialty Shops
A one-stop destination for tourist essentials and unique local products, offering a wide selection of handcrafted items and informative resources about Capiz.


Spanggo Foods, Cafe, and Pasalubong Center
Savor Capisnon culinary delights at this cozy cafe while browsing a variety of local delicacies and handcrafted pasalubong items.


Panulce
A haven for traditional Filipino baked goods, Panulce is known for its freshly made pastries and sweet treats that are perfect as souvenirs.


Mardebb Gift Shop
Find unique Capiz-made handicrafts, trinkets, and souvenirs, showcasing the creativity and artistry of local artisans.
