    San Antonio Resort – Your Chill Getaway in Roxas City

    Looking for a relaxing place to stay in Roxas City? San Antonio Resort might just be what you’re looking for. Tucked right along Baybay Beach, this 7-hectare property offers a peaceful escape with lots of space to breathe, unwind, and enjoy the sea breeze.

    Whether you're planning a weekend trip, a family vacation, or a beach wedding, we've got you covered. Our rooms are comfy and spacious, with options for solo travelers, couples, and big groups. Some even have balconies or patios where you can sit back and enjoy the view of our garden or lagoon.

    You can spend your day swimming in our pool, hanging out by the beach, grabbing good food at our restaurant, or even singing your heart out at karaoke. There’s plenty to do—or not do if you just want to chill. We also host events, so if you're organizing a party or meeting, we’ve got the space and the team to help you out.

    Only a few minutes from the airport and the city center, San Antonio Resort makes it easy to enjoy the best of Capiz—great seafood, friendly locals, and beautiful nature. Come over and feel right at home by the sea.

              Events & Catering
              Antonio's Hall

                The Antonio's Hall provides enough space indoors to accommodate 150 - 300 persons which is perfect for big events.

                Lakehouse 3

                  The Lakehouse 3 could accommodate up to 150 guests and may be extended to the lawn area. A venue which complimented by the picturesque view and romantic ambiance of the natural lagoon.

                  Lakehouse 2

                    The Lakehouse 2 has enough space for up to 110 persons and is tucked away from the main road.

                    Lakehouse 1

                      The lakehouse 1 can accommodate at 90 persons and may be extended outdoors to welcome up to 150 persons or bigger set-up.

                      Smaller event-spaces are available for a more intimate set-up or for corporate meetings. Outside catering may also be arranged.
