ESPACIO VERDE RESORT

About the Restaurant

Espacio Verde Resort offers two distinct dining experiences to cater to varied tastes: Abuelo Restaurant and Aquatico Restaurant.

Abuelo Restaurant serves as the resort's in-house dining hub, primarily known for offering a delightful breakfast to guests. Beyond breakfast, Abuelo also presents a selection of scrumptious cuisines adapted for the Filipino palate. It provides a comfortable and inviting ambiance, perfect for a relaxed meal.

Aquatico Restaurant offers a more vibrant, al-fresco poolside dining experience. Overlooking the resort's swimming pool and lush gardens, Aquatico specializes in serving classic Pinoy all-time favorite dishes. They also offer mini seafood surambaw on a per-reservation basis, allowing guests to enjoy this local specialty with advance booking. Additionally, Aquatico operates on a consumable rate basis, making it an ideal spot for enjoying a casual lunch or dinner with family and friends, complete with refreshing beverages from the poolside bar.
