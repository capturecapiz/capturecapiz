ACTIVITIES FOR THE MONTH OF FEBRUARY

February in Capiz

National Arts Month

Province-wide

In line with Proclamation No. 683, s.1991, February is a vibrant month for the arts in Capiz. The province showcases the immense talent of its local artists through various activities. Expect to see art exhibits in galleries and public spaces, film screenings, literary readings, and performance arts shows. Creative workshops are also organized, providing opportunities for both aspiring and established artists to hone their craft and collaborate.
