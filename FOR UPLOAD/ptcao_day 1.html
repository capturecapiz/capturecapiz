1-Day Tour Itinerary:

Embrace the Heart of Capiz

Explore the rich heritage, delightful hospitality, and vibrant local culture of Roxas City, the seafood capital of the Philippines. This carefully planned one-day tour is perfect for travelers looking to immerse themselves in history, savor fresh seafood, and experience the community’s charm.

Sites to be visit:

Visit the Heritage Sites
Discover the history and culture of Roxas City by exploring its
iconic heritage landmarks:

Birthplace of President Manuel A. Roxas
Visit the ancestral home of the first president of the Republic of the Philippines, a well-preserved heritage house showcasing artifacts and memorabilia from his life. This site provides insight into his legacy and role in shaping Philippine history.


President Manuel A. Roxas Monument
Located at the heart of the city, this statue commemorates the life and contributions of the city’s beloved leader. It's a perfect spot for a quick photo and reflection on his patriotism.


Roxas City Bandstand
Built during the American period, this charming structure is a historical and cultural landmark, often used for public gatherings and musical events. Its intricate design reflects the city’s colonial past.


Immaculate Conception Metropolitan Cathedral
This century-old cathedral stands as a testament to the city’s deep faith and resilience. Its Neo-Gothic architecture and spiritual significance make it a must-visit for devotees and history enthusiasts alike.


Sta. Monica Church and Panay Bell
Home to the largest church bell in Asia, the Sta. Monica Church in Panay is a masterpiece of Filipino craftsmanship. The awe-inspiring Panay Bell symbolizes faith and unity and is a highlight of any Capiz itinerary.


Ang Panublion Museum
Meaning “guardian of heritage,” this museum houses artifacts, historical documents, and cultural items that narrate the story of Capiz’s rich past. It’s a perfect spot for learning about local traditions and customs.

Savor the Capisnon Hospitality
Dive into a unique eco-tourism experience that showcases
the natural beauty and warmth of Capiz.

Palina Greenbelt Eco-Park / Cadimahan River Tour
Explore the tranquil waterways of Capiz through a scenic river tour. Bamboo rafts and charming views create a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.
- Experience the local way of life by trying your hand at fishing or harvesting oysters and green shells. Learn sustainable practices directly from the locals.
- Feast on a delectable seafood lunch while floating on a bamboo raft. Indulge in Capiz’s freshest offerings, including prawns, oysters, and fish, prepared in traditional Filipino style.
- Relax and rejuvenate with a soothing traditional massage provided by skilled locals. This therapeutic activity completes your experience of Capiz hospitality.




Commune with the Locals
Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and livelihood of the Capisnon people.

Dumolog Dried Fish Community
Witness the fascinating process of drying fish, a vital livelihood for many locals. This area highlights Capiz's importance as a seafood capital and allows you to interact with friendly residents.


Kapis Atbp.
Learn the intricate process of Capiz shell handicraft making through a live demonstration. These shells, known for their pearly luster, are crafted into stunning home decor, jewelry, and other products.


Banica Dried Fish Pasalubong Center
Shop for dried fish and seafood delicacies at this bustling center. A great place to buy affordable and high-quality souvenirs for family and friends.


Souvenir Shopping
End your day by collecting unique Capisnon products that capture the spirit of Capiz.

Capiz Tourist Information Center and Specialty Shops
This is your go-to destination for an array of locally made handicrafts and food products, showcasing Capiz's culture and creativity.


Spanggo Foods, Cafe, and Pasalubong Center
Enjoy freshly baked goods, traditional Filipino snacks, and local delicacies. Their wide selection ensures something for every palate.


Panulce
A haven for those with a sweet tooth! Discover delightful Filipino desserts and snacks that make perfect gifts or personal treats.
