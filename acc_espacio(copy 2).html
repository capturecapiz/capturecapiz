ESPACIO VERDE RESORT

    Espacio Verde Resort in Roxas City perfectly blends natural beauty with comprehensive amenities, ensuring a relaxing and enjoyable stay. You'll find 23 well-appointed suites, including Garden View and Lagoon View options, each offering modern comforts like air conditioning, private bathrooms, and complimentary Wi-Fi, with terraces for serene views.

    For dining, Abuelo serves delightful breakfast options alongside selected scrumptious cuisines, while Aquatico provides an al-fresco, poolside experience with classic Pinoy all-time favorite dishes for lunch and dinner, complemented by a poolside bar.

    The resort features an outdoor swimming pool with waterslides, providing recreational fun. Guests can also enjoy activities like kayaking in the lagoon or leisurely strolls through the lush gardens.

    Beyond leisure, the resort is a popular venue for various events, offering dedicated banquet halls (El Comedor and Cabida) and outdoor spaces suitable for seminars, birthdays, and weddings, all supported by comprehensive food catering services. For your convenience, the resort also provides complimentary airport and bus station shuttle services. Ultimately, Espacio Verde Resort's most defining feature is its tranquil and verdant environment, characterized by beautifully landscaped gardens, a picturesque lagoon, and a peaceful ambiance that truly allows guests to live closer to nature.
