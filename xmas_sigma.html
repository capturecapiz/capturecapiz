Capiz Christmas Festival 2024

    Brief Description

                This Christmas season, the Maayon Christmas Village is set to enchant visitors with a festive celebration that captures the true spirit of Christmas. At its heart is the Nativity Scene, reminding everyone of the season's real reasonâ€”Jesus Christ.

                The Paskong Pinoy celebration will feature unique, native designs, including parols crafted with local materials that reflect our cultural identity. We will incorporate natural elements like bamboo, coconut leaves, and coconut fibers, lending an authentic touch to the festivities. The Christmas Village will also showcase traditional bahay kubo (nipa huts) adorned with colorful lights, parols, and festive decorations, accompanied by the joyful sounds of Christmas hymns that evoke the warmth of Filipino homes during the holiday season. This space will serve not only as a celebration venue but also as an opportunity for community bonding, reinforcing the Filipino values of togetherness and generosity.

                The PASKWA ni JUONE Capiz event will have a transformative impact on the town of Sigma, creating a powerful sense of identity and pride that resonates throughout the community. By celebrating Filipino Christmas traditions through the Maragtas Christmas Village Competition, Sigma becomes more than a venue, it becomes a cultural beacon that draws locals and visitors alike to experience the distinct beauty of Paskong Pinoy.

                This event will not only boost tourism, but it will also deepen community ties, uniting residents in a shared appreciation of heritage. Such a celebration underscores Sigmaâ€™s role as the "Heart of Capiz," reminding all who attend of the richness of Filipino traditions and the warmth of Filipino hospitality. Through this event, we aim to foster a strong sense of community, honor our cultural roots, and create a joyful space where families and friends can share unforgettable moments. The Paskong Pinoy Christmas Village will not only be a delightful experience but also a tribute to the enduring spirit of Filipino hospitality and resilience.

                By celebrating the Christmas season, the Municipality of Sigma aspires to strengthen feelings of belonging and joy, connecting friends and family through cherished customs, beloved traditions, and the creation of new memories.
