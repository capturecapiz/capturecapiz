Jamindan Christmas Village
Capiz Christmas Festival 2024

    "Pangahaw Festival" Theme
    Eco-Friendly and Indigenous Decorations
    Traditional Filipino Parols
    Community Unity and Collaboration
    Festive and Heartwarming Atmosphere
    Brief Description

                The Jamindan Christmas Village brings to life the heartwarming essence of Filipino Christmas traditions, blending faith, culture, and community spirit into a mesmerizing holiday experience. This year’s concept focuses on the "Pangahaw Festival", celebrating Jamindan's abundant natural resources and the values that make the community unique.

                Christmas, a beloved holiday in the Philippines, is a time for family gatherings, delicious feasts, and spreading love and positivity. In Jamindan, this celebration transcends festive decorations and sparkling lights, becoming a powerful symbol of unity and cultural pride.

                Central to this celebration is the Filipino Christmas lantern, the Parol, which symbolizes the "Star of Bethlehem." The village features these iconic stars alongside decorations crafted from local materials like abaca, cogon, and rattan, reflecting Jamindan’s rich heritage and commitment to sustainability.

                The Christmas Village showcases Jamindan’s natural bounty through captivating displays, including mini-representations of local resources and products. This approach not only highlights the community’s creativity but also underscores the importance of eco-friendly and indigenous materials in fostering harmony with nature.

                Through this unique tradition, Jamindan transforms into a vibrant hub of joy, excitement, and shared experiences, uniting its people in the spirit of giving, sharing, and celebrating Filipino values. The village becomes a testament to the resilience, generosity, and cultural richness of Jamindanganons, offering a festive space for lasting memories and heartfelt connections.
