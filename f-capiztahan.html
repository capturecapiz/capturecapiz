Capiztahan

Celebration of the foundation of the Civil Government of Capiz and the rich history that defines our province.

About The Festival

Capiztahan marks the founding of the Province of Capiz, established on April 15, 1901, by the U.S Philippines Commission through Act No. 115.


The province now includes one city and sixteen municipalities across two districts. Capiztahan's highlight is the Maragtas sang Capiznon, a grand parade featuring elaborate floats and a dance drama competition,showcasing Capiz's rich history from pre-colonial to post-colonial times; and the Surambaw Seafood Feast.
