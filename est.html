Coco Veranda Bar and Restaurant

About the Restaurant

Coco Veranda offers a quintessential Capiz dining experience with its stunning beachfront location and a menu centered around the freshest catch from the sea. Known for its laid-back and relaxing atmosphere, it's the perfect place for family gatherings, romantic dinners, or simply unwinding with friends while enjoying the sea breeze. The restaurant prides itself on sourcing local ingredients to create authentic and flavorful grilled seafood and classic Filipino dishes.

Menu Highlights

  • Grilled Scallops with Garlic Butter
  • Crispy Pata
  • Sinigang na Hipon (Shrimp in Sour Broth)
  • Baked Talaba (Oysters)
  • Inihaw na Pusit (Grilled Squid)
