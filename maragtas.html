Your Gateway to Capiz

Welcome to the Maragtas Tourist Information and Pasalubong Center, your first stop in discovering the wonders of the province. Named after the legendary Maragtas epic, our center is dedicated to providing visitors with valuable information and a taste of local craftsmanship and delicacies. Whether you need tour assistance or want to find the perfect souvenir, our friendly staff is here to help.
