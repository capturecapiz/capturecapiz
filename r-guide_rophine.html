About Me

I am one of the DOT Accredited Regional Tour Guide with decade of experience, proudly serving the Province of Capiz. I am connected with Capiz Provincial Tourism and Cultural Affairs Office, actively contributing to initiatives that promote sustainable tourism and celebrate our local identity. My journey has been deeply rooted in the province’s rich cultural and natural heritage, guiding visitors through its stories, landscapes, culture and traditions with passion and dedication. Through the years, I’ve remained committed to promote Capiz as one of a must visit destination - where history, hospitality, and community converge.
