    Naulan Falls: A Refreshing Oasis in Dumalag

                Nestled within the lush, green landscapes of Barangay Concepcion in Dumalag,Naulan Falls offers a refreshing and accessible escape for those looking to cool off and reconnect with nature. This beautiful cascade tumbles down a wide rock face, creating a stunning curtain of water that flows into a natural swimming pool below.

                 The cool, clear waters of the basin are perfect for a relaxing dip, making it an ideal destination for families and friends looking for a fun day out. The sound of the falling water provides a constant, soothing soundtrack, creating a tranquil atmosphere where you can truly unwind.

                Known for its natural beauty and easy accessibility, Naulan Falls has become a beloved getaway for locals and tourists alike. It’s a perfect spot for a picnic, a swim, or simply to sit back and enjoy the simple, unspoiled wonders that Capiz has to offer. Whether you're seeking adventure or a peaceful retreat, Naulan Falls promises a delightful and rejuvenating experience.
