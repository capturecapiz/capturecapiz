Palm Coast Beach Resort

    Palm Coast Beach Resort – Your Everyday Destination #experiencePalmCoast

    Palm Coast Beach Resort is a cozy beachfront escape tucked away in the quiet, non-populated shores of Basiao. True to our tagline, “Your Everyday Destination,” we offer a place where guests can return again and again—for quick getaways, meaningful celebrations, or simple days of peace by the sea. We believe that relaxation, good food, and great service shouldn’t be reserved for special occasions alone. At Palm Coast, you can enjoy that everyday.

    Our hashtag, #experiencePalmCoast, invites everyone to be part of the unique charm we offer—whether it's waking up to ocean views, enjoying your morning coffee steps from the beach, or paddling over calm waters at sunset. Each visit is more than a stay—it's an experience you’ll want to relive.
