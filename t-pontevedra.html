    Main Features
    Known for rice and coconut farming.

    Rich cultural heritage, reflected in local festivals.
    Strong agricultural community.
    Historical landmarks that showcase the town’s past.
    Brief Description

                Pontevedra, located in the southern part of Capiz, is a town that heavily depends on agriculture, with rice and coconut farming being its primary economic drivers. The town is blessed with scenic rivers and coastal areas, making it an ideal destination for eco-tourism and nature-based activities. Pontevedra’s lush landscapes and agricultural abundance contribute to its reputation as a rural paradise.

                The town is also home to a strong cultural heritage, with local festivals that celebrate its history and traditions. The residents of Pontevedra take great pride in their roots and work hard to preserve their cultural practices. Pontevedra’s combination of agricultural prosperity, historical landmarks, and natural beauty makes it a significant municipality in Capiz.
