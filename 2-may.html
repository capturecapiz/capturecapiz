ACTIVITIES FOR THE MONTH OF MAY

May in Capiz

Sinaot Festival

President Roxas, Capiz

A vibrant festival celebrating the local culture and bountiful harvest of President Roxas, featuring street dancing and cultural presentations.

Lingga-anay Festival

Panay, Capiz

A festival centered around the town's famous giant bell, featuring vibrant street dancing competitions that narrate its history.

Guyum-Guyuman Festival

Pontevedra, Capiz

Celebrates the town's primary products like crabs and shrimps with colorful parades and culinary events.

Kaadlawan Festival

Roxas City

The charter anniversary of Roxas City, marked by various civic events, cultural programs, and celebrations throughout the city.

National Heritage Month

Province-wide

A month-long celebration of Filipino heritage, as per Proclamation No. 439, s.2003, with various activities highlighting Capiz's historical sites and cultural traditions.

Tagbuan Festival

Pilar, Capiz

A festival that celebrates the meeting and convergence of cultures ("tagbuan") in the historic town of Pilar.
