ACTIVITIES FOR THE MONTH OF AUGUST

August in Capiz

Buwan ng Wikang Pambansa

Province-wide

A month-long observance celebrating the Filipino language, with various academic and cultural activities held in schools across Capiz.

National History Month

Province-wide

Events and exhibits are held to commemorate significant historical events and figures from Capiz and the nation.

Padagyaw Festival

Dumarao, Capiz

"Padagyaw" means to make a joyful noise, and this festival is a boisterous celebration of the town's founding anniversary.

Pagpasidungog Festival

Panitan, Capiz

A festival of honoring ("pagpasidungog") the town's achievements and outstanding individuals.

Hinugyaw Festival

Cuartero, Capiz

A celebration of joy and revelry ("hinugyaw") that showcases the culture and products of Cuartero.
