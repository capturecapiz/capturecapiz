    Brief Description

                Dumalag is a serene municipality in the heart of Capiz, known for its harmonious blend of nature, history, and culture. The town’s natural attractions, from pristine springs and cascading waterfalls to lush greenery, provide an inviting escape for nature enthusiasts and adventurers alike. Its historical landmarks, including centuries-old religious structures, stand as testaments to the town’s enduring faith and rich cultural heritage.

                The community thrives on its agricultural roots, evident in the picturesque farmlands that surround the area. Dumalag also celebrates its identity through vibrant festivals and culinary traditions, offering visitors a warm and flavorful experience. With its tranquil charm and rich history, Dumalag is a gem waiting to be explored.
